Today I’ve been impressed by a Great Google Service..

Maybe someone already knows this service, but I’ve discovered it today by searching a way to have mail forwarding on my domain: alexworld.it

I’m searching a tiny pop3 daemon to forward my email address of my domain to my gmail.com’s email addres and I’ve discovered it:

http://www.google.com/a/help/intl/en/var_1b.html - Smart Apps for email, documents, calendar and more! a lot more!

By editing your DNS records you can use Google to manage your domain email addresses, custom domain names for: your calendar, your sites, your google homepage and more, more too!

I leave you some examples:

WPA_cli_applet for gnome panel!

WpaCliApplet (wpa_cli_applet.py) : is a Gnome Applet to monitor wpa_state and scanning of wpa_supplicant,
to work correctly it needs wpa_cli and wpa_supplicant running (if not running it will display status: NOT RUNNING)
Version: 0.2
Current version support tooltip to show more information about connected network,
in case of SCANNING state: it will display the Networks found.

Download it: wpa_cli_applet-0.2.tar.bz2

View the source code: wpa_cli_applet.py

View the bonobo server file: GNOME_WpaCliApplet.server
